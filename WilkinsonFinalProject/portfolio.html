Booklet

This is a collaborative booklet project I did in my infographics design class on gun violence. I was the art director for the project, so I created the style guide and lead our 16-person class to create this 20-page piece. I designed the front and back cover using Photoshop and Illustrator, and I collaborated with a partner to create the “Gun Violence in the Media” spread on Pages 16 and 17.
