My name is Lauren Wilkinson, and I’m a rising senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am in the School of Media and Journalism in which I’m specializing in editing and graphic design. I’m very passionate about the impact women have had on history and how women can empower other women in today’s world, particularly in the media industry.

I grew up on a farm in a small town in North Carolina called Oxford, of which a 2017 national champion (Isaiah Hicks) was born. I have always had a passion for art and design and knew when I was little that when I grew up, I wanted to be on a creative career path. Besides creating art and designing, I love to travel (my favorite places in the world are Charleston, South Carolina and

Florence, Italy), attack puppies that aren’t mine, put on an excessive amount of mascara, buy shoes that I don’t need, obsess over sunsets, sing like no one is watching me in my car car, and try new restaurants. This is my portfolio site, showcasing work that I’ve done either for a project, in my spare time, for a freelance job or during an internship, so enjoy!
